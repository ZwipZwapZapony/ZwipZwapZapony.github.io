I'm Zwip-Zwap Zapony

I'm a hobbyist video game modder

A video game "mod" is a "modification", changing or adding to the behaviour in the official release of a video game.

I find enjoyment in mods, as they allow me to experience a favourite video game in a different way, when and if I feel like it.

I primarily find myself modding four games recently, with quite a variety as to what types of mods I make depending on the game.

- With DOOM (2016) and DOOM Eternal, I typically make various miscellaneous gameplay tweaks, changing small aspects of the games in a modular fashion.
- With Minecraft: Java Edition, I mostly make personal resource packs, replacing the textures and models of a few blocks, items, and enemies.
- With Sonic Robo Blast 2, I sometimes delve into the source code of the game, fixing small bugs or adding features in hopes of them being implemented in an official update of the game.

You're probably here for the DOOM 2016+ Modding subpage, which relates to modding DOOM (2016) and DOOM Eternal.
